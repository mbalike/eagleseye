ABOUT ORGANIZATION

We awaken opportunity
through vision

Eagle’s Eye Foundation (EEF) is a registered NGO in Tanzania Mainland committed to empowering communities through knowledge, opportunity, and sustainable development.

Our mission is to empower youth, women, men, and vulnerable groups through education, skills development, engineering engagement, WASH initiatives, innovation, and economic empowerment.
